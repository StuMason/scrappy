Course Information

The Wisley Golf Club, Surrey is a 27 hole course with 3 loops of 9 hole courses; The Church, Garden & Mill Courses. A private, parkland golf course designed by Robert Trent Jonesin 1992.

Mike R rated 5 out of 5

Reviewed Jun 22 2021

Stunning golf course, very difficult

A very exclusive (you can only play as a member guest) private club, American-style layout with three nines. Conditioning is exceptional. Stunning scenery and expertly designed layout. It is very difficult although not long on the card. The generally soft fairways mean there is little roll so it plays long. Danger lurks everywhere. Very tight off the tee so brng your A game and play up a set of tees if you get a chance to play.

(Round played: Jun 2021 - Score: 84 Handicap:2.9)

Jonathan S rated 5 out of 5

Reviewed May 3 2019

What a course (and venue!)

A fully private members club, but lucky enough to have a client who is a member.

See Dennis Pugh and his star pupil on the practice range was a pretty good start to the day!

The 18 holes we played we exceptional in quality. Some real strategic holes which make you think hard about what shot to play.

Greens were running 10.5 on the stimp which made for some interesting putting, especially when downhill!

The clubhouse and the facilities are as you would expect from a modern private members club.

Get to know a member and try and persuade them to take you. You won't be disappointed.

(Round played: May 2019 - Score: 84 Handicap: 7.7)
